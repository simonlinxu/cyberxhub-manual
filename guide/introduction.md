# Introduction to CyberXHub

## 1.1 About CyberXHub
[cite_start]CyberXHub is dedicated to leveraging AI, IoT, and digital twin technologies to develop intelligent inspection products, build integrated inspection platforms, and deliver enterprise-grade solutions and services for industrial inspection and operations[cite: 15].

## 1.2 Platform Overview
[cite_start]CyberXHub is an integrated industrial inspection platform designed to unify fixed monitoring systems, mobile robotic platforms, and intelligent software workflows into a coordinated, scenario-driven solution[cite: 17]. [cite_start]By enabling cross-system integration and real-time collaboration, it empowers industries to digitize field operations and optimize decision-making[cite: 18].

### 1.2.1 Key Capabilities
* [cite_start]**Scenario-Based Workflow Deployment**: Supports energy inspection (solar, wind, electric grid), public safety, emergency response, and surveying/mapping[cite: 21].
* [cite_start]**Cross-System Coordination**: Connects fixed on-site monitoring (visible light, thermal, gas sensors) with mobile platforms (drones, wheeled robots, robotic dogs)[cite: 24].
* [cite_start]**Layered Architecture**: Employs a modular architecture composed of five logical layers: Acquisition, Transport, Platform, Application, and Industry Scenarios[cite: 33, 34, 36, 38, 40, 42].
